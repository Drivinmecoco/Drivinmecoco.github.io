ARTHUR AND THE DEMON CAT

A quest for revenge and adventure.

  • Insta

The story starts with the Demon Commander Scarlette wageing war on the Gods of the world. She decides to drink her super strength potion. But instead of giving her super strength, it turns her into a cute fluffy cat.

The cat is found by the Spring goddess and is adopted to be the pet of her son Athur. He is a sweet boy that dreams of grand adventures.

The cat repeatedly tries to escape without success and eventually convices the young boy to help her of her quest to find and punish the wizard who cursed her.

They need to travel all the way from Saturn the Harvet world to Mercury, the Demon World. On the quest they will encounter many obstacles.

On their first quest Arthur gets his arm bitten off by a monster.
